Under the RBI's Scale-Based Regulation (SBR) framework for NBFCs (effective October 22, 2021), the minimum Net Owned Fund (NOF) requirements are tiered by layer classification:

NBFC-Base Layer (BL): minimum NOF of ₹2 crore (for new entrants from 2022 onwards; existing NBFCs granted transition until March 2027).

NBFC-Middle Layer (ML) and Upper Layer (UL): minimum NOF of ₹10 crore. Investment and Credit Companies (ICC) and Microfinance Institutions (MFI) within the Base Layer also require ₹2 crore.

Infrastructure Finance Companies (IFC) categorised in the Middle Layer must maintain a minimum of ₹300 crore.
Affected teams: Compliance Risk Management Finance
